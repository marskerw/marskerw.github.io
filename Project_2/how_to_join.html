How to Join

Students can join on the Miami University HUB. After joining on the HUB, it will send an email to the 4 Paws management who will then send you an email. In order to be considered, students must adhere to these requirements:

Requirements

To be a foster the student must live off campus

Puppy-Sitters and general members can live on campus

In order to be a foster or a puppy-sitter you must go through an interview process with 4 Paws for Ability Miami
